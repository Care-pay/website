Mission

CarePay empowers healthcare providers and insurers through seamless, AI-driven claims processing automation, accelerating reimbursements and fostering trust between healthcare providers and insurers.

Vision

CarePay aims to establish a new era in African healthcare as the most trusted platform for automated claims processing, relied upon by insurers and healthcare providers for swift claim handling, prompt payments, and enabling providers to offer exceptional care free from financial constraints.

OUR PRODUCTS

CarePay offers an ecosystem of two main products. The first is MediMiddle, a middleware solution that facilitates data transmission between hospitals and insurance companies. It also includes a web portal for both insurance companies and hospitals to streamline their interactions.
